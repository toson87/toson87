## Minh-Son To

Clinician-Scientist · MD MBiostat PhD · Radiology Trainee (RANZCR) · Senior Research Fellow

I work at the intersection of **medical imaging, machine learning, and clinical translation**.

### Research Interests

- Oncological imaging and neuroradiology
- Foundation models and multimodal learning for medical imaging
- Clinical translation and real-world deployment of AI in healthcare
- Explainable AI — bridging theory and clinical usability
- Diagnostic test accuracy and evidence synthesis in medical imaging
